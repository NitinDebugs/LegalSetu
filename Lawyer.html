Types of Lawyers

Criminal Lawyer

Handles criminal offenses, defense, and prosecution cases.

Civil Lawyer

Deals with property disputes, contracts, and civil litigation.

Family Lawyer

Handles divorce, custody, marriage disputes, and more.

Corporate Lawyer

Manages business contracts, mergers, and legal compliance.

Cyber Lawyer

Specializes in cybercrimes, data privacy, and IT laws.

IPR Lawyer

Protects copyrights, patents, and trademarks.

Constitutional Lawyer

Focuses on constitutional rights and governance.

Tax Lawyer

Helps with tax disputes, returns, and compliance.
